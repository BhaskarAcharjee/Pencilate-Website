Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how Pencilate handles your data.

1. Information Collection

Pencilate does not collect any personal information unless explicitly provided by the user for feedback or support purposes. We respect your privacy and focus on delivering a fully offline experience wherever possible.

2. Local Storage

All your handwritten pages, settings, and customizations are stored locally on your device. Pencilate does not upload any content to external servers without your permission.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use services like Google Fonts or crash reporting tools only to improve app performance. These services may collect limited data governed by their own privacy policies.

4. User Permissions

The app may request permissions such as storage access only when necessary for features like saving PDFs or importing images. No data is accessed or used beyond the scope of intended features.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, feel free to contact us through the in-app feedback option or email us at support@pencilate.app.
